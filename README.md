## 👋 About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), dedicated to exploring the intersection of **cryptography** and **practical AI applications**. My research focuses on building efficient and secure Verifiable AI systems using techniques such as Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP), Homomorphic Encryption (HE), and Differential Privacy (DP).

My academic journey began during my undergraduate studies, where I focused on wireless communication networks under the guidance of Prof. Yi-Huai Hsu. During my Master's at NTUT CISLAB, advised by Prof. Yu-Chi Chen, I established my core expertise in privacy-enhancing technologies and cryptography. Currently pursuing my Ph.D. at NTHU, I am advised by Prof. Te-Chuan Chiu and co-advised by Prof. Yu-Chi Chen. I aim to transform the foundation of communications and cryptography into a secure backbone for future AI infrastructure.
